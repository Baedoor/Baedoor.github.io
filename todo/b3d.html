Baedoor is ultimately the game I dreamed about since childhood. The ideas from back then were polished and expanded over time, to eventually reach the conclusion that what I imagined as perfect RPG should be immersive in very subtle way - in great extent, Morrowind with its province mods and Ashfall being perfect example.
Morrowind aesthetical style and a lot of oldschool mechanics are also what draws me towards the idea of making my game very much follow similar design - and, maybe even make Morrowind plugins work with it as seamless experience?

Currently, the game is in early development stage where it is mostly worked on conceptually.
