Welcome in Baedoor Haven.
I'm Toma, or Toma400 as you may know me from other places. I love art in all forms and shapes, and so you will find variety of art in here - from games and mods, through poems or writing to music. But my most beloved child is my steampunk/fantasy universe - Baedoor.

Baedoor was made approximately in year 2004, and since then affects all my creations heavily. It is fantasy/steampunk universe, but unlike most of those, it isn't hero tale. It is story of civilisations trying to live their lives through everyday struggles, as well as cultural and political tensions.
The universe draws inspirations from many places, including such as Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind or Arcanum: Of Steamworks and Magic Obscura.

My lifetime dream is to release full 3D RPG game in this universe one day. And maybe few others - you can visit this page to read on them further.

If you are skilled in writing, concept art, pixel art, 3D modelling (especially lower poly) or coding, visit contributing page or Discord server to help me with creating this universe.
Anyone willing to just discuss and follow the project is also very welcomed!
