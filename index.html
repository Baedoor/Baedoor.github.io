Welcome in Baedoor haven. It's a hub for everything related to my steampunk/fantasy universe, and a bit of my own other art.

What is my plan, you would ask? What is even Baedoor to begin with?

Baedoor is my own universe, made approximately in year 2004. It consists of history of multiple civilisations, their cultural journey and the circle of life and death. It is fantasy/steampunk universe, but unlike most of those, it isn't hero tale. It is story of societies trying to live their lives through everyday struggles and tensions.
The universe draws inspirations from many places, including such as Elder Scrolls III: Morrowind or Arcanum: Of Steamworks and Magic Obscura.

My plan is to release full 3D roleplaying game in this universe one day. In years preceeding this dream of mine, several other games and ideas came, so my current goal is a bit wider - you can visit this page to read on them further.

If you are skilled in writing, concept art, pixel art, 3D modelling (especially lower poly) or coding, visit contributing page or Discord server to help me with creating this universe.
