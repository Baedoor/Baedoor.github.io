Contribute: Writing

Writing is currently the biggest helping task to prepare current two developed projects smoothly to their finishing stages.
Even though I consider myself fairly decent with writing, it is one of art fields that takes a lot of time to produce something of great quality. Additionally, English isn't my native language, which makes it harder for me to produce something of equal quality if I were writing in my own. And the amount of writing needed for all project is fairly massive.



IoA is game that relies incredibly heavy on writing, being text RPG. This means that it needs a lot of dialogues and writing that will fill its open world, ideally coming from different writers even.

  • ✮ Books
    Browse book requests on GitHub or suggest your own idea, based upon Baedoor lore. Books require good knowledge on universe, but I will also try to help as much as I can with that if needed.
  • ✮ Quests
    Browse quest list or suggest your own idea (ideally adding to areas where current design lacks). Your job would be to write down all the dialogues needed, including the ones changing with branching off of player's decisions.

Wastelands mod is in big need of Patchouli book with its upcoming updates. While not technically essential for release, it provides all required knowledge about the mod within game experience.

Contributor of this resource should know at least minimally Patchouli book system, to be able to fluently build the guide book. Knowledge on mod's contents (ideally from versions before 1.20.1) is also heavily appreciated, as it will make communication on resources much quicker.
