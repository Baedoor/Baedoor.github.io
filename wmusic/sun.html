Sun Tribe

Sun Tribe is small project that came out of my fascination with trance scene, especially projects such as ATB.
I wanted to give this sound a chance, and the method of puzzling sounds felt quite mesmerising. The idea of album came to me.
While the album never came out eventually, the idea of trance project still sits in my mind, so the project may get reactivated when good time comes.
