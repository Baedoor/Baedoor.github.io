Rat 41

Rat 41 started as my personal blog where I was writing reviews of games, music, and potentially also movies and books - though the two latter never came to existence.
Eventually, with a bit active few first years, the project became stale, with me only posting AOTY posts each year - summing up my favourite music albums.

Currently, I'm in the process of moving Rat 41 to new format - of Internet magazine, or zin as some may recall such publication genre.
First edition of Rat 41 zin should be made for December - including 2024 AOTYs, a couple of reviews, and new sections I would like to introduce for the new format.
The zin will be distributed via website and be bilingual.
Ideally, I'd like to release two editions per year, but the amount will depend on amount of time I will have. Stay tuned for more news!
