Spires of Baedoor is second Baedoor mod for Minecraft, which similarly to Civilisations branched off of ideas that were meant to be included in Wastelands originally - but were seen as too broad for steampunk mod to include.
Spires were made to include Baedoor magic in the world of Minecraft, and also as outcome of fascination with Electroblob's Wizardry and other 1.12 magic mods I really liked. Additionally, with time I also decided that there could be more fun way to play with herbalism, so the second aspect of the mod would be to have more in-depth gameplay for gathering, researching and using plants.

While currently on hold, Spires is considered as one of mods I still want to do at some point in the future. Some ideas conceptualised for the mod were actually reintroduced in Baedoor lore - so even if the mod were to never publicly appear, the ideas on magic will probably do in all Baedoor games planned.
