WW2

Latest version: 0.9

Archives

WW2 is my first ever mod I was making back in 2012, for Battle of Wesnoth game. It adds several factions and tons of units related to World War II - or, to be more specific, taken from World of Tanks franchise.

The genesis of the mod is quite silly, as I made it to motivate my father to play with me - he is great fan of strategies, yet I couldn't convince him to play BfW because of its fantasy vibes. Therefore the mod idea was born.

Progress Status

WW2 wasn't touched by me since 2012 or 2013 - its last version was a while ago. I wasn't really keeping good organisation back then, therefore now it's hard for me to go back and immediately know what to do.
However, I was working on new version for the mod back then, shuffling some units around and making balance changes. I wanted to add some new mechanics, as well as Italian and Japanese units then - nowadays, with World of Tanks having those nations, the latter is even easier.

I will at some point finalise this "forever-indev" update. Once this is done, I want to do major overhaul for the mod, drawing all units, so it is possible for me to not feel guilty for using copyrighted material.
In addition, this will make the mod be able to stand on its own legs - without any legal caveats, it will be added to BfW mods system, and just have nice dedicated assets.
It is a plan for later time, though - I want to finish more projects before I derail myself to the ones that are already playable.
