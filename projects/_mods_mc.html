Wastelands of Baedoor

Mod implementing variety of new steampunk-related features into the game. Includes several new weapon types - such as firearms, sabres and throwing knives, as well as airships and ormath civilisation.

Civilisations of Baedoor

Mod adding new dimensions related to Baedoor universe, with unique cultures living there. In addition, it allows player to make their own empire from ground up, hiring armies.

Mod focused on magic, inspired by Electroblob's Wizardry. Additionally, it features brand new alchemy system based upon herbalism.
