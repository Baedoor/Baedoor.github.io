Add-ons


Wastelands of Baedoor has some of its features supported by datapacks system. While mod authors can include those in their works to easily integrate their mod with WoB, there's also a Google Drive repository that allows you to download them as optional content. The repository is maintained by me (Toma), so you can also bring any issues found on Discord server for me to fix.

Wastelands of Baedoor - Oh The Biomes You'll Go: Woodpact


Author: Toma400

Woodpact is integration mod between Wastelands of Baedoor mod and Oh The Biomes You'll Go. It allows for BYG's new wood types to have variants introduced by WoB, such as pillar, lamps or stairs/slabs.
